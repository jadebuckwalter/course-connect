CRLS Course Connect Mentor Form

If you are accessing this form, you have previously signed up to be a mentor for CRLS Course Connect. Please fill out the form below.










The purpose of this form is to enter the names of courses that you have taken, so that students can email you to ask questions about these courses.

To find a list of courses that you have taken at CRLS, log in to Aspen, go to "My Info", and click on "Transcript." Click on the funnel button and select "All Records." Enter as many or as few courses as you like; just know that you should feel comfortable answering questions about any course that you enter.

Search for courses below, and add them to your list of courses. If you need to remove any of the courses on your list, click on the name of the course. When you're done, hit submit.
