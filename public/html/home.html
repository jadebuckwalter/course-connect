About CRLS Course Connect

CRLS Course Connect is a website where CRLS students can search for courses they are considering signing up for and get more information by connecting with mentors who have taken those courses.

Writing an email to a mentor

When writing an email to a mentor, here are some things you might want to include:

  • Start by introducing yourself; say your name and what grade you're in. If you already know your mentor through a particular class, sport, or extracurricular, feel free to introduce yourself this way as well.
  • Tell your mentor which course you are considering, and ask your questions.
  • Thank your mentor. They should write back to you within a few days.

Your final email may look something like this:

Subject: CRLS Course Connect Question

Hi [mentor name],

My name is [your name], and I am in [your grade]. I am thinking about signing up for [course name] next year, but I am wondering [ask your questions].

Thank you!
[your name]

What types of questions should I ask?

You can ask your mentor all sorts of questions, but they may be able to answer some questions better than others. Here are some good questions to get you started:

  • How heavy is the course workload? How many hours per week did you spend on homework and/or studying for the course?
  • What types of assignments did you have to complete for the course? Were there a lot of projects? Essays? Tests? Was it a discussion-based class?
  • What did you enjoy most, or least, about the class? Would you recommend it to someone like me?
  • Did this course lead you to become interested in something new?
  • What courses did you take after this course? What does the course trajectory look like?

Please refrain from asking questions about individual teachers. When you sign up for the course, you don't get to choose the teacher, and your mentor might not even have the same teacher as you.
