About CRLS Course Connect

CRLS Course Connect is a website where CRLS students can search for courses they are considering signing up for, and connect with mentors who have taken those courses.

Writing an email to a mentor

When writing an email to a mentor, here are some things you might want to include:

  • Start by introducing yourself by saying your name and what grade you're in. If you already know your mentor through a particular class, sport, or extracurricular, feel free to introduce yourself this way as well.
  • Tell your mentor which course you are considering, and ask your questions.
  • Thank your mentor. They should write back to you within a few days.

Your final email may look something like this:

Hi [mentor name],

My name is [your name], and I am in [your grade]. I am thinking about signing up for [course name] next year, but I am wondering [ask your questions].

Thank you!
[your name]
